Rabbit's Intelligent Move



Once there lived a frog in a pond which is at a small distant from a village. Near to that pond a Rabbit is living in a small pit. They both become very close and meet daily to share their feelings eachother. Their dayt will not be completed unless they meet each ther. One day they both went for a walk in the evening. Suddenly the Frog fall into a hole nearby. Frog got panic and he don't kbnow how to come out of that hole. "My friend! Please do something. Help me to get out of this hole." frog asked Rabbit. Hearing those words Rabbit said " Don't get panic this is just a small hole use your strength and make a jump you will be out of it." But Frog is not in a condition to listen those words. Rabbit suggested the frog to hold the stick and come out from it.


Agitated Frog saw a small snake hanging there. At the same time Rabbit said "immediately come out of that hole otherwise the snake will bite you and you will be dead." On hearing this all of a sudden the Frog jumped out of that hole and saved himself from the snake and survived. Then Frog said "See my dear Friend without giving a try you stood there for my help. To let you know your own strength i placed a wire there and played a game like this." On saying this Rabbit took a wire from the hole After that Frog thanked Rabbit who save his life from the danger.


Moral of the Story: The Moral of the story is "Don't wait for anyone to help you. Know what your strengths are and save yourself from the danger. Think wisely to get out of the trouble.

Lion's OverSmartness

Long Ago there lived a Lion in a Jungle. Everyday it went out to hunt animals to eat. One day it started hunting animals but unfortunately on that day it cannot find any animal to kill. Then the lion has no idea about what to do and he thought if it happens daily it wont get food to eat.


After sometime the oversmart Lion make a cunning plan for hunting. One day a Fox came to meet the Lion in the jungle. To the Fox the Lion said " My health condition is not good. I don't know how many days i will leave. I wish to see all the animals." and requested the Fox to spread the news to all the animals. Knowing that it was true Fox spread the nows to everyone. From the next day one-by-one animals went to see the Lion in his cave.


One day two rabbits are standing at the entrance of the cave looking inside. Fox intended to make a visit to the Lion's cave and on watching those two rabbits fox started conversation with them. In the middle of the conversation Fox knew that Yesterday a Deer went into the lion's cave and didn't come out till now. Thinking about this Suspecious activity Fox understood the plan of the Lion and ordered those two rabbits to run away from that place.


After sometime Lion came out of the cave and saw the Fox standing at the entrance and invited him inside. The smart Fox insisted that by saying that there are many footsteps towards inside the cave and none of them came out , after they come out i will come inside. The Fox also exclaimed "Ahh! See the Deer is coming out now". Stunned Lion look back to see the deer. Taking this chance the Fox ran away from the Lion to save his life.


Moral of the Story: Don't trust anyone too blindly. Some may help you. Some may threaten you. Not everyone is good and trust worthy.

A crow become A Peacock

There is a crow who hates himself for his voice and his appearence. It always compares with others in all situations and felt bad. It always blamed God for giving such a life. One day the Crow was flying over a jungle and saw few Peacockfeathers laying there in an open place near a tree. Seeing those feathers Crow felt happy and an got an idea. As the crow doesn't like his appearence it want to change his appearence as a Peacock.


The Crow then stick those feathers to it's tail and wandering round the jungle. At that time an old crow saw him and scolded the crow for his behaviour and said "Whatever you do. You can't change yourself at the end you are a crow." Even though the crow listened his words he negleted and joined with the Peacock group.


At the same time the climate become pleasent and it starts raining. All the Peacocks flickup their feathers and starts dancing and singing. On seeing them crow also started singing as Ka-Ka-Ka . All the peacocks shockingly stare at crow as they didn't know how the crow joined their group. Those peacocks pluck the feathers of the crow and make him ran away.


The old crow again saw the crow and said " You are always a crow and you cann't change it. Stay happy and be happy with your friends and family." The crow now understood that and again joined with his family.


Moral of the Story: Be happy with what you have. Don't rush for the things that you didn't get and loose your happiness.
